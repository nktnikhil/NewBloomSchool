Diverse Curriculum

At New Bloom High School, we offer a diverse curriculum that includes traditional academic subjects like English, Math, Science, and Social Studies, as well as courses in Art, Music, and Physical Education

Project-Based Learning

At New Bloom High School, we believe that students learn best when they are actively engaged in the learning process. That's why we offer a range of project-based learning courses that allow students to apply what they're learning in a real-world context.

Language Immersion

We believe that learning a second language is an essential part of a well-rounded education. That's why we offer language immersion courses that allow students to develop proficiency in a second language while also learning about the culture and customs of that language

Management Technology

This is a full time course offered by the Department of Management Technology with avision of expansion and mobilization of facilities for offering good quality management education and research in the country. To make sure a holistic management education and to assist the graduates develop into modern leaders of the future, the Institute provides possibilities outdoor the lecture rooms as well.
